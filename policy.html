Everyday App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20 May 2020

Welcome to Everyday! Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding this information.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Everyday is designed to be your go-to app for day-to-day tasks without compromising your privacy. We do not collect or store any personal data from our users. The application offers three types of lists for your convenience:

  • Shopping List: Helps you keep track of items to buy.
  • Guest List: Allows you to manage guest details for events or gatherings.
  • Expense List: Enables you to record and manage expenses.

2. How We Manage Your Information

Since we don't collect any personal data, we don't use or share your information with anyone. All lists created within the app are stored locally on your device and remain private to you.

3. Data Security

Your data security is our priority. Lists and data created within the Everyday app are stored locally on your device. We do not utilize cloud storage or external servers, ensuring your information stays with you.

4. Suitable for All Ages

Everyday is a user-friendly app designed for everyone. There are no age restrictions for using our app. We maintain a safe and secure environment without the need for personal data collection.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be communicated by posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

6. Contact Us

Your feedback and concerns are important to us. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at everydayfeedback@gmail.com.
